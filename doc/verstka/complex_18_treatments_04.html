Luhacovices natural healing resourse such as mineral springs, peloid, natural healing gases, local climate as well as rehabilitiation techniques and procedures allow physicians to tailor made individual and effective treatment programmes for people who suffer from various health problems related to respiratory diseases, diseases of the digestive organs, circulatory cardio-vascular illnesses, metabolic disorders and locomotive apparatus disorders. At once this programme could be used for regeneration of the whole body.

Examples of Specialization:
Cure for Diabetics – the treatment is dedicated for all types of diabetes; it is concentrated on all problems, which this disease brings (incl. laboratory screening, profile of blood sugar and special diet).

Respiratory tract regeneration – in case of the upper respiratory disease, a part of the cure is an otorhinolaryngological examination and a phoniatric consultation, in case of the lower respiratory diseases, a part of the cure is spirometry.

Mamma programme – recovery stay for women after operation of breast cancer after completion of treatment – in complete remission. For entry medical examination the ladies are asked to bring their medical documents from their cancer specialist.

Programme Includes:

  • Meet and Greet at the airport of your arrival: Brno, Prague, Vienna, Bratislava and Transfer to Spa Hotel Miramare.
  • Entry Medical Examination.
  • 18 treatments individually tailored by your physician according to your medical condition.
  • 13 nights /14 days, Full Board stay in Standard or Standard Superior room at Spa Hotel Miramare.
  • Medical Examination at the end of your stay.
  • Transfer to the airport of your departure: Brno, Prague, Vienna, Bratislava.

Not Included:

  • Personal Travel Insurance.
  • Spa Fee of 15 CZK per day.
  • Supplements and Optionals.

Terms:

  • Minimum course duration - 14 nights
  • Commencement date – every Sunday.
  • Treatments - Monday to Saturday.

Luhacovice City is a hidden gem of south-eastern Moravia, region rich in ancient castles and temples of the XVI century. Because of its miraculous waters, friendly, mild climate conditions, fresh air, beautiful natural scenery, and people Luhacovice is one of the world's most famous spa resorts.
